📋 How to get your M-Pesa statement:

  1. Dial *334# on your Safaricom line
  2. Select My Account → M-Pesa Statement
  3. Choose the period (e.g., Last 3 months)
  4. Enter your email address
  5. Check your email for the statement
  6. Download and upload the file here

Supported formats: PDF CSV TXT
